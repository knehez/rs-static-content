WHO WE ARE

THE COMPANY

RisingStars Asia, a Singapore company, was established in 2011 as an online karaoke service provider company with its mobile application and website, launched in the Philippines. It spent three years developing its online and mobile karaoke platform which has the ability to produce TV quality sound and video recording even on average capability smartphones. Based on this technology, the RisingStars TV show is a game changer to the conventional talent show genre. It is the first to incorporate online talent search and instant audience challenge into a televised singing competition. In November 2014., after signing a partnership contract with the Philippine TV5 Channel, the first talent search has started with a lots of twists and suprises. RisingStars is the first TV show that captures the mobile trend into a TV show format.

RISINGSTARS APPLICATION

The Risingstars mobile app is available for free download from Google Play, suitable for all type android smartphones. It is the only available karaoke app customized for the Filipinos and this is the main foundation – stone of the RisingStars talent search.

RISINGSTARS WEBSITE

The website supports the mobile app and provides another convenient way to the users browsing among the hundreds of songs and recording the selected ones.

SOCIAL MEDIA

The company’s vision is to connect the karaoke funs and so establish a RisingStars community. Besides the social functions on its own website as sharing, commenting, liking, joining lounges, RisingStars has an active Facebook page, Youtube channel and a Twitter account as well. Turning to social media from the RisingStars application, contestants can reach out to thousands of friends and followers to gain support and collect popular votes online. RisingStars seamlessly merges the power of television, mobile technology, and social media, capturing the imagination of the ‘selfie generation’ who so far shunned traditional television programming.
