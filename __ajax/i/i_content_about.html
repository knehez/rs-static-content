THE COMPANY

Rising Stars Asia is a Singapore based media company. Established in 2011, Rising Stars began by hosting online singing contests through its internet karaoke and competition platform. The next step was adding broadcast-quality mobile application recording, and many unique competition features, including a fully integrated TV show format. The technology developed by Rising Stars has become a complete game changer in the conventional talent show genre, which captures the current smartphone trend, and seamlessly integrates it with not only the TV show, but the most popular social media platforms as well. The first season of RisingStars Philippines was aired nationwide on the TV5 Network, Saturdays at 10 pm, starting in mid March 2014, and running for 10 weeks. The programme was hosted by celebrity Ogie Alcasid, with Miss Universe runner up Venus Raj as his partner.
