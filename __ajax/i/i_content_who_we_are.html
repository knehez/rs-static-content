WHO WE ARE

THE COMPANY

RisingStars Asia, a Singapore company, was established in 2011 as an online karaoke service provider company with its mobile application and website, launched in the Philippines. It spent three years developing its online and mobile karaoke platform which has the ability to produce TV quality sound and video recording even on average capability smartphones. Based on this technology, the RisingStars TV show is a game changer to the conventional talent show genre. It is the first to incorporate online talent search and instant audience challenge into a televised singing competition. In November 2014., after signing a partnership contract with the Philippine TV5 Channel, the first talent search has started with a lots of twists and suprises. RisingStars is the first TV show that captures the mobile trend into a TV show format.

RISINGSTARS APPLICATION

The Risingstars mobile app is available for free download from Google Play, suitable for all type android smartphones. It is the only available karaoke app customized for the Filipinos and this is the main foundation – stone of the RisingStars talent search.

RISINGSTARS WEBSITE

The website supports the mobile app and provides another convenient way to the users browsing among the hundreds of songs and recording the selected ones.

SOCIAL MEDIA

The company’s vision is to connect the karaoke funs and so establish a RisingStars community. After the karaoke videos have been uploaded users - turning to social media - can reach out to thousands of friends and followers to gain support and collect likes by sharing their recordings on facebook, youtube or other social media platforms. RisingStars seamlessly merges the power of television, mobile technology, and social media, capturing the imagination of the ‘selfie generation’ who so far shunned traditional television programming.

TALENT SEARCH

The turn key RisingStars mobile & online platform complete with on ground contest system makes it easy and inexpensive for everybody to act on their dream and to join the contest anytime anywhere!

All entries gathered from a certain region goes through a series of screenings, eliminations and live auditions that culminates to a regional Mall Show. Entries submitted from various cities or provinces are uploaded on the RisingStars website into separate Regional Contests. Entries are judged by professional Jurors and RisingStars users by voting on their favorite performances. Contestants can increase their chances by sharing their competition entries on Facebook and other social media sites.

The Jury selected best ones competes in a live, onstage competition hold at the biggest and most visited city or provincial malls – in all big cities across the country. The top two winners in the live competition at the Mall Show gets to the next big – on air - phase of the competition.
