Privacy Notice

Personal Information

Browsing RisingStars You can listen to songs and watch videos on RisingStars without having a User Account. You can also contact us by emailing us directly, without having to register for an account.

User Account.For some activities on RisingStars, such as uploading or downloading karaoke songs or videos, posting comments, voting for favorite songs, accessing for fee only services, you need a User Account. We ask for some personal information when you create an account, including your email address, country of residency and a password, which is used to protect your account from unauthorised access.

Usage Information. When you use RisingStars, we may record information about your usage of the site, which other users you communicate with, the songs you listen to, the videos that you watch, the frequency and size of data transfers, as well as information that you click in RisingStars. If you are logged in, we may associate that information with your User Account. In order to ensure the quality of our service to you, we may place a tag (also called a "web beacon") in HTML-based customer support emails or other communications with you in order to confirm delivery.

Content Uploaded to Site. Any personal information or video content that you voluntarily disclose online may be collected and used by others.

Uses

If you submit personal information to RisingStars, we may use that information to operate, maintain and improve the features and functionality of the site and to process any other communication that you send to us.

We do not use your email address or other personal information to send commercial or marketing messages without your consent. We may use your email address without further consent for non-marketing or administrative purposes (such as notifying you of major RisingStars changes or for customer service purposes).

We use cookies, web beacons and log-file information to: (a) store information so that you will not have to re-enter it during your visit or the next time that you visit RisingStars; (b) provide custom, personalised content and information; (c) monitor the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns; (d) monitor aggregate metrics such as total number of visitors and pages viewed; and (e) track your entries, submissions and status in promotions, sweepstakes and contests.

Information That is Publicly Available

When you create a User Account, some information about your account activity will be provided to other users of RisingStars. This may include the date that you opened your User Account, the date that you last logged in to your User Account, your age (if you choose to make it public), the country and the number of songs and/or videos that you have listened to, watched or uploaded.

Your User Account name, not your email address, is displayed to other users when you engage in certain activities on RisingStars, such as when you upload songs, videos or send messages through RisingStars. Other users can contact you by leaving a message or comment on the site.

Any songs or videos that you submit to RisingStars may be redistributed through the Internet and other media channels and may be listened to / viewed by other RisingStars users or the general public.

You may also choose to add personal information which may include your name, gender, profile picture or other details, that will be visible to other users on your User Account public profile page. If you choose to add certain features to your User Account public profile page, then these features and your activity associated with these features will be displayed to other users and may be aggregated and shared with other users. Such shared activity may include your favourite songs and videos, songs and videos that you rated, songs and videos that you have uploaded.

Your Choices

If you have a User Account, you may update or correct your personal profile information, email preferences and privacy settings at any time by visiting your account profile page.

You may control the information that is available to other users at any time by editing your User Account and the features that are included on your public profile page.

You may, of course, decline to submit personal information through RisingStars, in which case you can still listen to songs and view videos and explore RisingStars, but RisingStars may not be able to provide certain services to you.

Advertising on RisingStars

RisingStars strives to provide you with relevant and useful advertising and we use cookies as well as other third-party, ad-serving systems to do so. We may use a range of information, including cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, usage data and other non-personal information about your computer or device (such as browser type and operating system) to provide you with relevant advertising. While you are logged in or logged out of your User Account, we may also show you advertising based on non-personally identifiable information that you have provided to us in your User Account.

Advertisers may serve ads based on interests and demographic categories associated with non-personally identifiable online activity, such as songs listened to, videos viewed, frequency of uploading or activity on other advertising partner sites. Advertisers may also serve ads to you based on previous activity on that advertiser's website or based on non-personally identifiable information from other companies. This non-personally identifiable information is held separately from your User Account data or other personally identifying information in our possession.

Other third-party ad-serving companies may display ads on RisingStars. RisingStars does not provide any personal information to these third-party ad servers or ad networks. Any data obtained by third-party ad servers subsequently shared with RisingStars are maintained and dealt with by RisingStars in accordance with this privacy policy.
