From phone to fame - the RisingStars TV Show


Creighton Strategies Ltd. has created U-STAR, the game changer talent show Format, that was first aired in the Philippines in 2015 under the name of RisingStars Philippines. U-STAR is the first singing talent show that allows TV viewers from the comfort of their homes to challenge the contestants in the TV show and replace them in the competition. The particularity of our format versus any other formats before is the opportunity for the audience to jump into the competition from any place via the dedicated U-STAR mobile application. Each week the judges select the best challenger that comes through the mobile application to give him/her the opportunity to go on stage for the live audience challenge with the TV finalist of his/her choice creating inexhaustible twists and turns . U-STAR keeps alive the popular „audition feeling” throughout the entire season and add a fresh touch as new faces show up in every new Audience Challenge Episode.
