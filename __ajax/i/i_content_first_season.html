FIRST SEASON

RisingStars Philippines is a televised singing talent show production, which was aired on Channel Tv5 and the web and mobile platforms of www.risingstars.ph, produced by RisingStars Asia Online Pte. Ltd.

FROM PHONE TO FAME: STREAMLINING THE AUDITION PROCESS

The goal of the Production is to find amateur singers with talents of singing and special characteristics that enables them to perform in front of a big audience and that they can entertain millions with their performances. Amateur singers submit their favourite song as a contest entry on the free karaoke platform www.risingstars.ph or the free RisingStars android app downloadable from Google Play. For those who lack internet access and/or who are not familiar with the mobile technology, RisingStars set up recording booths in well visited Malls nationwide at pre-announced time periods, where RisingStars staff assisted in recording the performances.

RisingStars has revolutionized the talent search turning the smart phone into the key element of the audition process and the whole show itself - by utilizing the mobile technology, the web and social media. No more lines, no more casting call, all people need is talent, confidence, an android smartphone and internet connection to audition.

No talent show has ever been able to reach out to so many people as easily as RisingStars with its top quality mobile app that turns regular smartphones into centrally connected karaoke machines.

After the entry videos have been uploaded, contestants - turning to social media - can reach out to thousands of friends and followers to gain support and collect votes by sharing their unique facebook link in the competition. RisingStars seamlessly merges the power of mobile technology, social media and the TV.

CHALLENGE

To keep contestants on their toes and create a real drama in the show, anyone from the audience is able to challenge a contestant by singing the same song through their smartphones. Running under the (trademarked) slogan “I can sing better than that”, challengers can submit their version of the song via the RisingStars website or mobile app. The best of those challenges submitted, as selected by the judges, is then invited to join the live contest, and if they end up winning against the contestant they've challenged, they will then have the opportunity to continue as a regular contestant. With this format which keeps the contest wide open, anyone with true talent has a chance to enter the competition, even at the last minute, just by using their mobile phones, true to the “from phone to fame” concept.
