RisingStars Talent Show, 2014/2015 Season
The first season of the RisingStars talent show was aired on TV5 from mid of March 2014. Having more than 200 stops in 20 cities, listening to more thousands of talented on-line applicants, 24 finalists were fighting through 10 weeks for the first prize. By the end of the show the professional jury along with the audience has discorvered new, bright, unadulterated stars.
