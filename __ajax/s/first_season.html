First Season

On a beautiful Saturday evening, a star was born. Last May 23, RisingStars Philippines wrapped up Season 1 proudly, as it crowned its Ultimate RisingStar.
After going through months of competition, a short presentation chronicled the journey of the Top 4 to where they were now standing. Together with host Ogie Alcasid, co-hosts Mico Aytona and Venus Raj, Rocelle, Krezia, Lee’Anna, and Remy performed a medley of song and dance numbers. Along with other former RisingStars, it was the perfect way to start the championship night.
