RisingStars Talent Show, 2014/2015 Season

The first season of RisingStars Philippines was aired nationwide on the TV5 Network, Saturdays at 10 pm, starting in mid March 2015, and running for 10 weeks. The programme was hosted by celebrity Ogie Alcasid, with Miss Universe runner up Venus Raj as his partner.
