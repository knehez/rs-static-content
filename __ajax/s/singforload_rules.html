RULES

All registered RisingStars users, who upload and share (on Facebook) their performance, as well as accept the competition rules, are eligible to participate in the „Sing and share for load” competition. It is necessary to have a Smart / TNT number. The top 5 ones having the most social activity on their performance will receive a guaranteed prize of a Php 300 Load weekly.

Promotion period: 2015 November 11 – December 9

Point summary of the top 5 performances

Abbreviation rs v.: # of votes on RisingStars, rs c.: # of comments on RisingStars, social: Facebook(shares, comments, likes)
Total= rs v. + rs c. + socail

2015 November 25 (6.a.m.) – 2015 December 1 (10. p.m.)

How can I join?

Make sure you have Smart and TNT number

Start to use the RisingStars Mobile App or visit www.risingstars.asia/ph/

Login with your Facebook account

Click the Competition tab and enter the competition lounge ’’Sing and share for Load”

Click on the "Enter Competition" button and accept the competition rules

Select a song from the appearing list

Start recording, then review and finalize the song

Upload your performance

Share it on Facebook by using the share button

Encourage your friends to like, share and comment your performance!


Voice only recordings are not accepted. You may revoke your entry from the competition after recording if you wish. To revoke your entry, go to „my recordings” tab in the mobile app or write an email to support@risingstars.asia a with the subject "Revoke competition entry".

How can I win?

By singing your best on the RisingStars mobile app or website. Sharing your performance on your Facebook and collecting likes, shares, comments from your friends, relatives or other RisingStars users. Each week during the promo period, the owners of the top 5 performance which has the most social activity in total, receive a Php300 Load each. The most social activity = Facebook likes, shares, comments + RisingStars app likes + RisingStars web likes within a week period. List of winners will be published on the RisingStars webpage / News on a weekly basis (each week on Thursday morning) as well as they will be contacted directly by the RisingStars team.

Prizes

Each week during the promo period, the owners of the top 5 performance
which has the most social activity in total, receive a Php300 Load each.
Prizes will be sent directly to the winners via sms.

How many songs can I upload?

All participants may upload only one song per week during the promotion period (4 songs in total).

May I join with one of my previously recorded song?

No. Each participant may join to the competition only with a new performance.
