RULES

All registered RisingStars users, who upload and share (on Fb) their performance, as well as accept the competition rules, are eligible to participate in the „Sing and share for load” competition. It is necessary to have a Smart / TNT number. The top 5 ones having the most social activity on their performance will receive a guaranteed prize of a Php 300 Load weekly.

Promotion period: 2015 November 11 – December 9

Weekly Cut Off:

2015 November 11 (8.a.m.) – 2015 November 17 (10. p.m.) 2015 November 18 (8.a.m.) – 2015 November 24 (10. p.m.) 2015 November 25 (8.a.m) – 2015 December 1 (10. p.m.) 2015 December 2 (8.a.m) – 2015 December 9 (10. p.m.)

How can I join?

Make sure you have Smart and TNT number

Start to use the RisingStars Mobile App or visit www.risingstars.asia/ph

Login with your Facebook account

Click the Competition tab and enter the competition lounge ’’Sing and share for Load”

Click on the "Enter Competition" button and accept the competition rules

Select a song from the appearing list

Start recording, then review and finalize the song

Upload your performance

Share it on Facebook by using the share button

Encourage your friends to like, share and comment your performance!


Voice only recordings are not accepted. You may revoke your entry from the competition after recording if you wish. To revoke your entry, you need to write an email to support@risingstars.asia a with the subject "Revoke competition entry".

How can I win?

By singing your best on the RisingStars mobile app or website. Sharing your performance on your  Facebook and collecting likes, shares, comments from your friends, relatives or other RisingStars users. Each week during the promo period, the owners of the top 5 performance which has the most social activity in total, receive a Php300 Load each. The most social activity = FB likes, shares, comments + RS app likes + RS web likes within a week period. List of winners will be published on the RisingStars webpage / News on a weekly basis (each week on Thursday morning) as well as they will be contacted directly by the RisingStars team.

Prizes

Each week during the promo period, the owners of the top 5 performance 
which has the most social activity in total, receive a Php300 Load each. 
Prizes will be sent directly to the winners via sms.

How many songs can I upload?

Participants may upload as many songs as they wish during the competition. It is possible that one person wins all the Loads, if he has the top 5 performances within a week.
