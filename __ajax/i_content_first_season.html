FIRST SEASON

The first season of the talent show was aired on the Philippines TV5 starting mid of March 2014, Saturdays primetime.

Afterwards RisingStars jury has listened to more thousands of online applicants, the most talented ones had a chance to introduce themselves live, during the Mall Shows, in almost 20 cities all around the Philippines.

The TV Show has started with 24 finalists, who were fighting through 10 weeks for the first prize. The bests were selected by the combined votes of the jury and the audience.

The show was hosted and juried by local celebrities and introduced completely new talent show elements as the „challenge” or the „free like ticket”.

CHALLENGE

All the contestants during selected weeks of the live show could be challenged by the audience or by contestants eliminated in previous rounds. The challenger could decide who (possibly all of them) to challenge, by singing and uploading the same song that the challenged contestant had in the previous live show on the “Challenge” platform of the RisingStars website or mobile app until a previously defined deadline.

RISINGSTARS WEBSITE

The website supports the mobile app and provides another convenient way to the users browsing among the hundreds of songs and recording the selected ones.

SOCIAL MEDIA

The company’s vision is to connect the karaoke funs and so establish a RisingStars community. Besides the social functions on its own website as sharing, commenting, liking, joining lounges, RisingStars has an active Facebook page, Youtube channel and a Twitter account as well. Turning to social media from the RisingStars application, contestants can reach out to thousands of friends and followers to gain support and collect popular votes online. RisingStars seamlessly merges the power of television, mobile technology, and social media, capturing the imagination of the ‘selfie generation’ who so far shunned traditional television programming.

Following the entry, the challenger could collect votes on the RisingStars website. The ones with the most votes had a chance for a battle with the selected contestant in the live episode. In case of a successful challenge, the challenger became regular contestant on the show.

FREE LIKE TICKET

The contestant, who got the most votes on the internet portal of the show, was automatically qualified for the next round in the contest, as the favorite of the internet audience.
